TEAM ARION

WE RACE

Team Arion is the official Formula Student racing team from Nitte Institute of Technology, driven by passion, precision, and performance. Dedicated to designing, engineering, and building high-performance race cars, the team proudly competes at SUPRA SAEINDIA, one of India's premier student motorsport competitions. With a blend of innovation, teamwork, and technical excellence, Team Arion represents the spirit of future mobility and the relentless pursuit of speed.
